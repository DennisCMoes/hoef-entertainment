*HalloWiebe* is a Dutch family-friendly Halloween comedy film, set to premiere in theaters across the Netherlands on September 24, 2025 . Directed by Jasper ten Hoor and Ivan Hidayat, the movie stars Duncan Meijering as Wiebe, a security guard tasked with overseeing an empty amusement park on the night before Halloween. He assembles an unconventional team—including his elderly landlady, a delivery driver, and a dinosaur—to assist him. However, their seemingly simple assignment takes a turn when they encounter three towering, destructive robots intent on demolishing the park
