*Heads Up* is a series written and directed by Siddharth Chandrasekaran. The project features Magalakshmi Anantha in the lead role.The first episode, called “Hide and seek”, has a psychological horror genre, which is heavily VFX driven. Featuring computer generated monsters and environments, which enhance the scary concept of the story.
